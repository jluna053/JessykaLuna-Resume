Getting to participate in these kinds of events has allowed me gain experience by working with others cooperatively.

I am currently a first year undergraduate student at the University of California Riverside. I decided to major in Sociology because I always wanted to understand how society works. Throughout my high school years, I was intrigued by people and their interactions. I began to question why people acted they way they did and why society was formed the way that it was. Being able to understand how society works and why people have different perspectives I am looking forward to learning more about.
